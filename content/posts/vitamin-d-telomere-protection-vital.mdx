# Vitamin D Reverses 3 Years of Biological Aging in Landmark Trial

## Key Findings

- 1,054 participants tracked for 4 years
- 2,000 IU/day vitamin D3 reduced telomere loss
- Equivalent to 3 years of biological aging protection
- Omega-3 showed no significant effect
