# Women Build Muscle on 5mg Weekly Rapamycin (Men Need 10mg)

## Key Findings

- 10mg weekly dose increased lean tissue in women
- 5mg dose improved general health in both sexes
- No benefit for visceral fat (primary endpoint)
- Generally well-tolerated at low doses
