70 million Americans struggle with sleep. Most resort to:

- **Ambien/Lunesta:** Addictive, tolerance builds, dangerous side effects

1. **Binds to benzodiazepine receptors** (GABA-A) in the brain
2. **Produces calming effects** without addiction potential
3. **Reduces sleep latency** (fall asleep faster)
4. **Improves sleep architecture** (more deep sleep, better REM)
5. **Zero tolerance buildup** (works long-term)

- **Reduces time to fall asleep by 24 minutes**
- **Increases total sleep time by 38 minutes**
- **Improves sleep quality scores 47%**
- **No next-day drowsiness** (unlike pharmaceutical sleep aids)
